This is Old Beijing chicken Roll history
Step1:Add salt and warm water to the flour and knead to form a smooth dough. Proof for 10 minutes.
Step2:Cut the chicken breast into strips, add shallot, ginger, cooking wine, soy sauce, and Orleans marinade, mix and marinate for 30 minutes
Step3:The proofed dough is kneaded into long strips.
Step4:Then use the panel to divide it into two doughs and form a circle.
Step5:Then press into the shape of a pie with your palm, and then brush a layer of cooking oil on the surface.
Step6:Cover with another pie, the two overlap.
Step7:Use a rolling pin to roll into a pancake.
Step8:Fry until slightly charred on both sides, then separate the two.
Step9:The marinated chicken breast is coated with starch.
Step10:Cook the oil in the pan to 180 degrees, and put the powdered chicken breast strips. Deep fry the golden cooked fish to remove the oil.
Step11:Take a slice of crepes, brush evenly with sweet pasta sauce, put lettuce, sliced ​​cucumber, chicken breast, and green onion.
Step12:Roll up and cut in half to start eating. Finally, you get home cooking old Beijing chicken roll.
